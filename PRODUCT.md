# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mineguai is for four-player mahjong groups who need a fast local score ledger at the table, primarily on mobile and often with one-handed operation between rounds.

## Product Purpose

The product records wins, losses, player names, step values, current balances, undoable actions, and session summaries in the browser. Success means a user can identify the opponent and record a hand in two quick taps without losing trust in the running totals.

## Brand Personality

Focused, tactile, restrained. The interface should feel like a high-quality digital table object: dark, deliberate, and calm under pressure.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic white dashboards, template-style Chinese ornament, heavy cyberpunk neon, fake full-rule mahjong engines, login-first products, and decorative landing pages that push the actual ledger below the practical task.

## Design Principles

- Speed at the table beats decorative complexity.
- The score surface must always show who is selected, what will be recorded, and how to undo.
- Visual richness should come from material, contrast, and hierarchy, not clutter.
- Local-first behavior is a product promise: data stays in the browser and the app remains static-deployable.
- Motion and sound must confirm action, never slow down accounting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, large mobile tap targets, reduced-motion fallbacks, and no reliance on color alone for win/loss or selected states.
